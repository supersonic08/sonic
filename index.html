Sonic Select's

Sci Fi Elements

Sonic is from a fake planet called Planet Mobius, and the book involves technology that does not exist. There are evil robots and an evil dinosaur robot. Lots of sci fi books have humans. In Sonic Select, there's Dr. Robotnik - he's the enemy.
